How LifeQuest works (everyone)

LifeQuest is a proficiency-based money game. Children progress through 6 levels and 48 stages, not by age alone — screening and quiz mastery set the path.

Lesson loop (strict): Read → Quiz → Game → Reward. The game cannot be “won” without understanding the lesson concept.
  • Currency: All real-money examples use AED (UAE dirhams). In-app “coins” are game rewards, not dollars.
  • City: Each completed stage builds a plot in your personal city.
  • Economy: XP, coins, streak, and LQ score are server-authoritative — the app never fakes rewards.
  • No competition: No leaderboards or addictive loops (school-safe design).

Level 1 · Coin Keeper — stage names

StageTitleWhat they learnTypical reward
1.1Counting the CostAdd up prices in AED before spending+10 coins
1.2The Trade-offChoosing one thing means giving up another+10 coins
1.3Needs Before WantsMust-haves before fun extras+15 coins
1.4Work and Get PaidEarning AED through jobs and effort+15 coins

Levels 2–6 follow the same pattern with more stages (L2=6 … L6=11). Unlocking the next level requires completing all prior stages and 80% average quiz mastery, plus time gates on higher levels.

Kid app What you see & do

Tabs

  • Home — streak, next stage, city preview
  • Learn — City map (default) or list view; tap a stage to play
  • City — full map, tap plots to build / replay lessons
  • Awards — quest ladder L1–L6, badges, stats
  • Me — profile, wallet, parent gate

City map (Learn tab)

Stages appear on a zigzag path over your skyline. Green check = done; highlighted card = your current stage. Tap an unlocked stage to start the lesson loop.

Lesson game

After each quiz, kids play Lemon City — set a price in AED, serve customers, and learn profit (price must stay above cost).

Design & quality rules

  • Colours: brand green #28B77F, gold #FFB018, teal info #00BEAA — no off-brand purple/navy in kid UI.
  • Animations: spring press (0.96 scale), celebrate on rewards, reduced-motion respected.
  • Games teach one concept each; Lemon City requires price > cost to win (profit).

Testing checklist

  1. Learn tab → City map shows Level 1 stages 1.1–1.5 with correct titles.
  2. Tap stage 1.1 → Read → Quiz → Game (Pick the Deal) → Reward.
  3. City tab → plot builds after completion.
  4. Parent dashboard loads linked child without permission errors.
  5. Run flutter test — all unit tests pass.
